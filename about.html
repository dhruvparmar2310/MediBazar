Our Story

We started with a simple thought of providing affordable and accessible healthcare services for all in the country. Founded by Dr.Darshan Parmar & Dhruv Parmar in 2020, MediBazar+ began with providing services in just Call on Order.

You can order online medicines and wellness products, can call with Experts Doctors and Read Articles too for which sample is collected at your home/office. Currently, we serve 10 million customers and provide healthcare services.

Our Vision is, any customer can see every products details, medicine description from Home & also can read Articles of there choice.

Improve health outcomes for consumers by making it simple, personal, accessible and affordable

Our Mission is to be Earth's most Customer centric Online Medical platform; to build a place where people can come to find & discover any Medicines & Healthcare Products they might want to buy online.

Use technology to make MediBazar+ the trusted partner for 100+ million customers for all of their healthcare needs.
